List of Documents for Admission

1) an application addressed to the rector;

2) a copy of the identity card (3 copies);

3) notarized copies of education documents with attachments (bachelor / specialist diplomas and master / graduate residences);

4) a notarized copy of passing english test certificate, TOEFL - not less than 460 points, IELTS - not less than 6.0 (if available);

5) a receipt for payment through the Halyk's Bank of the RK for the entrance examination in a foreign language (except for those who have provided a foreign language certificate, TOEFL, IELTS);

6) list of scientific and methodical works;

7) a letter of recommendation from a place of work or study at a higher educational institution on an official form (may be in the form of an extract from the meeting of the Academic Council of the Faculty / University);

8) medical certificate by form No. 086-U with fluorography, certified in the Students' Polyclinic of Almaty city or the Medical Center of IITU;

9) personal sheet on personnel registration (original) and a notarized copy of the document confirming work experience;

10) an extract on the availability of pension contributions for the period of time indicated as a time of work (only for citizens of the Republic of Kazakhstan, who are enrolled for training under the state educational order);

11) 6 photos in the size 3x4;

12) copies of author's certificates and awards received in the process of training, as well as documents confirming participation in olympiads, contests and competitions, including international, republican, scientific (if available);

13) letter-direction from the organization on the official form (for persons entering the targeted training program).

Admission to the specialized school IITU is developed by the School annually under the Model Rules of admission to educational organizations that implement educational curriculum of basic secondary, general secondary education of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Acceptance of applications begins after the presentation of the document of the state sample on the level of basic secondary education.
